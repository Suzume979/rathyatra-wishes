The Divine Chariot Festival

Dear Devotee,

As the sacred chariots roll through the streets, may Lord Jagannatha's divine grace shower upon you and your loved ones.

On this auspicious occasion of Rathayatra, may you experience the boundless love of the Lord of the Universe. May your life be filled with the same joy that devotees feel when they pull the ropes of the celestial chariot.

Let the journey of the Trinity - Jagannatha, Balabhadra, and Subhadra - remove all obstacles from your path and illuminate your spiritual journey.

May your heart become the chariot that carries the divine presence wherever you go.

Jai Jagannath! Jai Balabhadra! Jai Subhadra!
